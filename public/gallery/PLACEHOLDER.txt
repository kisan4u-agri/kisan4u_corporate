This is the dedicated folder for the Kisan4U Media Gallery.

You can drag and drop your 37 images into this folder:
- File names should be: 1.jpg, 2.jpg, 3.jpg, ..., 37.jpg
- They will be served in the application at the path: /gallery/1.jpg, /gallery/2.jpg, etc.
- The gallery will automatically pick these up and present them with beautiful captions and categories!
